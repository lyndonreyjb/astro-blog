# Metal Madness Pedalboard

This board is built for metal. The **Boss MT-2 Metal Zone** is the centerpiece, paired with a **Noise Suppressor NS-2** for clean, aggressive tones. For additional versatility, there's a **Dunlop Cry Baby Wah** and **MXR Smart Gate**.
